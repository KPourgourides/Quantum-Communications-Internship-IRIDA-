# Relevant States & Operators

>Convention: $\hat{X}_{\theta} = \hat{a}e^{-i\theta} + \hat{a}^{\dagger}e^{i\theta}$

## Fock States $\ket{n}$

- Eigenstates of the number operator $\hat{n}\ket{n} = \hat{a}^{\dagger}\hat{a}\ket{n} = n\ket{n}$

- Definite photon number $(\Delta \hat{n})^2 = 0$

- Undefined phase $\phi$

- Probability distribution of number of photons is Poissonian

- Wigner function is circular and centered at the origin of the phase space

## Coherent States $\ket{a}$

- Eigenstates of the annihilation operator $\hat{a}\ket{a} = a\ket{a}$

- $a$ is the complex amplitude of the state; $a = |a|e^{i\phi} \to$ well defined **optical phase of EM field**

- In the Fock basis $\ket{a} = e^{-\frac{|a|^2}{2}}\sum_n^\infty\frac{a^n}{\sqrt{n!}}\ket{n}$

- Average photon number $\braket{a|\hat{n}|a} = |a|^2$

- Generated by displacing the vaccuum $\ket{a} = \hat{D}(a)\ket{0}$; $\hat{D}(a) = \exp(a\hat{a}^{\dagger} - a^*\hat{a})$

- Wigner function is circular, displaced by distance $2|a|$ from the origin and at an angle $\phi$ in the phase space

## Squeezed states $\ket{z}$

- $z = |r|e^{i\theta}$, where $r$ is the squeezing parameter

- Generated by squeezing the vaccuum $\ket{z} = \hat{S}(z)\ket{0}$; $\hat{S}(z) = \exp\left[\frac{1}{2}(z*\hat{a}^2 - z\hat{\alpha}^{\dagger 2}))\right]$

- $\theta$ determines **which quadrature $\hat{X}_{\theta}$ is squeezed, not the same as the optical phase $\phi$ of the coherent state**. The field of a squeezed vaccuum **does not** have a well defined optical phase

- Squeezing the quadrature $\hat X_{\theta}$ results in the anti-squeezing of quadrature $\hat X_{\theta + \pi/2}$

$$(\Delta \hat{X}_{\theta})^2 \propto e^{-2r}, \quad (\Delta \hat{X}_{\theta + \pi/2})^2 \propto e^{2r} $$

- Average photon number $\braket{z|\hat{n}|z} = \sinh^2r$

- Wigner function is elliptical, centered at the origin and rotated by $\theta/2$ in the phase space

## Displaced Squeezed States $\ket{a, z}$

- Generated by displacing a squeezed vaccuum $\ket{z, a} = \hat{S}(z)\hat{D}(a)\ket{0}$

- Previous points for displaced and squeezed states hold independently

- Average photon number $\braket{z, a|\hat{n}|z, a} = |a|^2 + \sinh^2r$

- Wigner function is elliptical, displaced by distance $2|a|$ from the origin, is at an angle $\phi$, and rotated by $\theta/2$ in the phase space
